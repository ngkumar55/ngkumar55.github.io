Academic and Research Achievements

He taught agricultural Entomology to Undergraduate and Postgraduate levels. He worked in the field of cotton and soybean pest management programs. He initiated the systematic investigation on the subterranean termite, Odontotermes horni Wasmann (nest architecture, ecological aspects and role in agro-forest ecosystem). Apart from this, he also screened several newer insecticides against termites in the agro-forest ecosystems. He systematically investigated soil mesofauna's ecological and conservation aspects in agro-forest ecosystems for 16 years in a long-term fixed plot. He developed a methodology of mass multiplication of soil mesofauna of natural grassland and forest ecosystems and introduced them to the degraded agro ecosystem. He has published over 130 research papers and five book chapters. He has guided 25 M.Sc. (Agri) and 5 Ph.D. students.

Other Contribution

He is currently Hon. Secretary of the Indian Society of Soil Biology and Ecology. He organized two national symposia (2013 &2016) as Organizing Secretary.
